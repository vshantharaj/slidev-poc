Critical Success Factors

Computable Truth

Agents are only as good as your docs. Subjective "vibes" lead to hallucinations. Structured data leads to execution.

The 5-Step Rule

Limit initial agents to workflows of 5 steps or fewer before requiring human approval to prevent cascading logic errors.

Absolute Observability

You cannot govern what you cannot see. Use OpenTelemetry to trace agent "thoughts" like microservices.
